Jaisalmer's Mojo Jojo

The Powerpuff Girls | 1998/07/13

Mr. XYZ is dummy person from Jaisalmer, Rajasthan. Born in Gujrat, he has lived in Bamgalore but recently returned to his homecountry to travel. He loves walking around hidden backstreets discovering quirky shops and random art.

I love wandering through the streets of Jaisalmer, finding new places popping up at every corner. The city is constantly reinventing itself which was one of the reason I came back to my home city after many years. When asked about my experience, I can only respond:

Jaisalmer is the city of my dreams: This unique living urban centre, formerly an ancient fort, is home to over 3000 people with shops, people and cows in honeycombed old houses on criss-crossing lanes. Walk past temples and shops and stop for a cup of masala chai on a rooftop restaurant for spectacular panoramic views. Perfectly shaded yellow sandstone walls take you back to a time of battles and reinforcements here.

Get friendly with a local family in Jaisalmer,getting to know more about their traditions and culture, as they give you a quick MasterChef session on Rajasthani food. Dishes could vary, depending on the season you’re visiting in, from millet flatbread (bajreki roti), legumes (fali) and stuffed pepper (mirchibada) to lentil-infused hard wheat rolls (daalbaati). Enjoy your very own home-cooked Rajasthani meal with the family and don’t forget to take notes.

I hope that more and more people will see the true beauty of Jaisalmer soon. It is a captivating beast that you won't be able to take your eyes of again. Explore the city in your own pace and discover things you have never seen before.
